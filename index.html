Search SNPs



The T2D SNP Explorer allows you to search for genetic variants and selection statistics related to Type 2 Diabetes (T2D). You can search by SNP ID, Gene Name, Chromosome Region, or Population to retrieve relevant genetic data.

What Can You Explore?

  • SNP Details: Chromosome location, allele frequency, p-values, Genomic Coordinates.
  • Gene Annotations: Functional terms, associated diseases.
  • Population-Level Selection Statistics: FST, iHS, allele frequency.

How to Search?

Simply enter your search term, select a search type, and view results instantly. Use the dropdown to choose a search type:

  • SNP ID: Example - rs12345
  • Gene Name: Example - TCF7L2
  • Chromosome: Example - 10
  • Genomic Coordinates: Example - 10:100000-500000
  • Population Abbreviation: Example - SA
